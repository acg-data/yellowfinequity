About Us

We're on a mission to make business transitions seamless, profitable, and respectful of the legacy you've built.

Our Story

Why We Started Yellowfin

Yellowfin Equity was founded with a simple belief: business owners deserve better when it comes time to sell. Too often, sellers face high fees, impersonal processes, and buyers who don't understand the value of what they've built.

We set out to change that. By connecting owners directly with exceptional, vetted buyers who respect their legacy, we've created a process that's simple, confidential, and completely free for sellers.

Based in Austin, Texas, we've helped businesses across 20+ industries find the perfect buyer match—often with sellers maintaining ownership stakes for continued growth.

Our Values

What We Stand For

Partnership First

We view every transaction as a partnership. Our success is measured by your success in finding the right buyer for your business.

Confidentiality

We understand the sensitive nature of selling a business. Every interaction is handled with the utmost discretion and privacy.

Respect for Legacy

We honor what you've built. Our buyers are selected not just for their capital, but for their commitment to preserving your company's culture and values.

No Seller Fees

We believe sellers shouldn't pay to find the right buyer. Our service is completely free for business owners.

Long-term Thinking

Many of our sellers maintain ownership in the new company, achieving a "second bite of the apple" as the business continues to grow.

Excellence

We work with exceptional businesses and exceptional buyers. Quality over quantity is our guiding principle.
